ABOUT US

The event 'TENET' is an ambitious and noteworthy attempt of the GDSC team to create something never heard of. It is a new annual flagship event of GDSC, Chitkara University, where innovators get an opportunity to merge their creative ideas with their technical skills to build something exemplary.

Event Legs

Flutter

Flutter

Just like our hit program "Android Study Jams", here's another 3 day event where you get to learn the basics of Flutter, the fastest and most popular mobile app development framework. Followed by a workshop on Flutter, you'll be able to build a simple app with the help of the framework. And then complete on the final day of the leg

GAMING

GAMING

The event is a platform for the young and the young at heart to showcase their gaming skills. We not only want coding enthusiasts to showcase their skills but also to showcase their passion for gaming. This event will be equally marked as the other coding/non-coding legs.

BLOCKCHAIN

BLOCKCHAIN

On a very basic level, we will be introducing the concept of Blockchain. Followed by a one day workshop on Blockchain and then on the final day of the event, you'll compete with the knowledge you gained.

UI DESIGNING

UI DESIGNING

Design is at the heart of every business. It is the most important part of the business. We will be introducing the concept of UI Designing. Followed by a one day workshop on UI Designing and then on the final day of the event, you'll compete with the knowledge you gained. Infact, the best certificate design will be used as our main hackathon certificate.

WEB EXPLORATION

WEB EXPLORATION

The web is a powerful place. Since no one can argue with kindness, we wanted to create a challenge that would harness that power and result in web apps that not only look good, but also are useful. Following the theme of the event, we will be introducing the concept of Web Exploration. Followed by a one day workshop on Web Exploration and then on the final day of the event, you'll compete with the knowledge you gained.

DEBATE/PUBLIC SPEAKING

DEBATE/PUBLIC SPEAKING

Non-coding is a very important part of the event. This is what makes this hackathon unique. We want to make sure that everyone has a chance to speak out and make a difference.

COMPE PROGRAMMING

COMPE PROGRAMMING

In partnership with our codechef chapter, we will be introducing the concept of Competitive Programming. Followed by a one day workshop on Competitive Programming and then on the final day of the event, you'll compete with the knowledge you gained.

TECH QUIZ

TECH QUIZ

And the final event of the hackathon is Tech Quiz. This is the ultimate test of your skills. You will be given a series of questions and you have to answer them correctly. Only the top few participants will be selected for the final round.
